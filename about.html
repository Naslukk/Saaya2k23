About The Upcoming Show

Join us for an unforgettable celebration of culture and creativity at Saaya, the much-anticipated cultural fest happening at our college on November 23-24, 2023! Get ready to immerse yourself in a whirlwind of vibrant performances, captivating exhibitions, and an electric atmosphere that promises to leave you mesmerized. Saaya is not just an event; it's an experience that showcases the diverse talents and passions of our student community. Whether you're a music enthusiast, a dance aficionado, or someone who simply appreciates the richness of cultural expression, Saaya has something special for everyone. Come and be a part of this two-day extravaganza, where the spirit of unity and creativity converges. Let's create lasting memories together and make Saaya 2023 a celebration to remember! See you there!
